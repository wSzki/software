Instructions for Offline Authorization
--------------------------------------

Here’s what you need for offline authorization:

- An external storage device (such as a USB stick)

- A computer with an internet connection

- Your Ableton serial number (if you bought Live in a store)

- Your hardware code: 2249-6F5F-69F5-3259-ECDA-2145

On an internet-connected computer, go to www.ableton.com/authorize.
This web page will provide you with more information.
